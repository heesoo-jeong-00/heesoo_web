AltiAir

Branding / Packaging Design

The AltiAir project develops a brand offering a unique product: bottled air from the world's most iconic mountains. It features a distinct logomark that visually encapsulates a mountain, sunrise, and ground, complemented by a unique color palette inspired by the natural hues of the mountain regions and the countries where these peaks are located.

Beam

Branding / Identity System

The project 'Beam' is a rebranding initiative for a home goods store in Brooklyn, New York. While retaining the store's unique original brand identity, the project seamlessly integrates a new aesthetic to strengthen the brand identity.

Hang-It-All

Type Design

"Hang-It-All" is a type design project inspired by the Bauhaus wall-rack, Hang It All. The letters adhere to consistent rules that are connected to the shape of the original item, resulting in a unique display typeface.
