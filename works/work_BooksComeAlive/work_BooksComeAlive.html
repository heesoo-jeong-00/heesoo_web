Redesigning the user flow of the Verizon app

The Bookscape Project, guided by the message “Books Come Alive,” is focused on developing an interactive tablet application designed to provide an engaging and immersive reading experience for children. The app aims to bridge the gap between traditional storytelling and modern digital interaction by offering a dynamic platform where children can explore stories in a fun and interactive way.

App Mission

Bringing the warmth and interactive spirit of The Kid's Bookstore to a digital space, our e-book app offers young kids a fun, intuitive, and engaging way to explore fairy tales.

User Stories

Meet our users! Young readers love exploring new stories, and parents need a quick, reliable way to find the right books. How can we create a seamless, engaging experience that makes discovery effortless for both?

Wireframes

Let's dive into wireframes. Imagine a young reader excited to explore, but too many buttons, text, or a complex site map get in the way.

So, we designed simple, intuitive pages that let kids jump straight into their next adventure with playful, interactive elements.

Final Design

Your adventure now begins. Tap, explore, and dive in—where every click brings stories to life. Here, books aren't just read, they come alive!
