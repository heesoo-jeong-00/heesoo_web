Ink AI

  • Interaction Design
  • AI Integration

"Designing a new intelligent note-taking system."

Ink AI combines the familiarity of handwriting with seamless AI support to connect meeting preparation, live transcription, and follow-up into one continuous flow. This project focuses on defining a simple yet scalable structure and integrating AI naturally into the user experience, transforming fragmented notes into an intelligent workflow.

Why, with so many modern tools, does meeting and class note-taking still feel fragmented — and why does handwriting on digital devices remain awkward?

Ink AI's UX design began with this question, aiming to seamlessly integrate human note-taking and AI assistance into one intuitive flow.

Key insight from the research was that users don't just need a smarter note tool — they want an integrated experience across the entire meeting flow.

From this, we defined three design goals and explored them through hand sketches and Crazy 8 exercises.

In the wireframing stage, we explored various layouts and interactions.

Usability testing revealed the necessity of preserving the familiarity of handwriting while allowing AI to act as a natural, unobtrusive assistant, which guided us to continually refine the interactions.

Key Learning: Effective AI UX is not about adding more features, but about appearing naturally at the right moment in the user's flow.

Ink AI was a 0→1 creation, defined by a simple yet scalable structure with AI seamlessly integrated into the UX. It demonstrates how meetings can shift from fragmented records to seamless, intelligent experiences, with future opportunities in shared notebooks, multi-device sync, and calendar/communication integration.
