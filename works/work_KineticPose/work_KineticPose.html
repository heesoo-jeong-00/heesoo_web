"Interactive Art: Shaped by Your Motion."

Experience the captivating allure of "Kinetic Pose", a web project brought to life with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You can explore the dynamic circles that follow your every move. Inspired by the principles of magnetism and gravity, this project creates a dynamic and mesmerizing experience, allowing users to explore the interaction between their movements and the digital environment.
